Analysis Phase 

The Analysis Phase is when you understand what the software/system needs and decide what it should do. 

The Output of Analysis stage: SRS (software requirement sheet) document describing all system requirements. 

Three roles in the analysis stage: 

* Product Owner – defines the product goals and communicates with stakeholders. 

* Business Analyst – gathers and analyses requirements. 

* Project Manager – plans, manages timeline, and coordinates the team.
